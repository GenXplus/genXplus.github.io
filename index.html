We are Generation X

The Forgotten Yet Powerful

We are the first wave of the digital revolution, but we still remember life before online. We grew up with Star Wars and were defined by X files. We are namesakes with the spin-off of X-men, designed to reflect the complexity of our demographic. GenXers contributions to society include Space X, Google, Amazon, Wikipedia and Youtube. Before any of us existed, the term Generation X was used to denote a ‘generation of young people about whose future there is great uncertainty’ But now we are a bellweather for the future.
